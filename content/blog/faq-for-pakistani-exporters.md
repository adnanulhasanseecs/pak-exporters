## Pricing and Payment Questions

### Q: How do I handle international payments?

**A:** While Pak-Exporters facilitates connections, payment terms are negotiated directly with buyers. Common methods include:
- Bank transfers (TT/Wire transfer)
- Letters of Credit (L/C)
- PayPal (for smaller orders)
- Escrow services
- Trade finance options

**We provide guidance** on international payment methods and can connect you with payment service providers.

---

### Q: What currency should I use?

**A:** Most international buyers prefer:
- **USD (US Dollars)** - Most common
- **EUR (Euros)** - For European buyers
- **GBP (British Pounds)** - For UK buyers

**Best practice:** List prices in USD, but be flexible to negotiate in buyer's preferred currency.

---

### Q: How do I set competitive prices?

**A:** To set competitive prices:
- Research competitor prices on the platform
- Review RFQ winning quotes
- Consider your costs and margins
- Factor in shipping and handling
- Consider volume discounts
- Adjust based on market demand

**Remember:** Price isn't everything. Quality, service, and reliability also matter.

## RFQ Questions

### Q: How does the RFQ system work?

**A:** The RFQ (Request for Quotation) system works like this:
1. Buyers post their product requirements
2. You see RFQs in your product categories
3. You submit competitive quotes
4. Buyers review quotes and select suppliers
5. You connect with selected buyers

**Benefits:**
- Qualified leads (buyers are actively looking)
- No cold calling needed
- Direct access to buyers
- Competitive bidding opportunity

---

### Q: How do I win more RFQs?

**A:** To increase your RFQ win rate:
- Respond quickly (within 24-48 hours)
- Submit detailed, competitive quotes
- Highlight your unique advantages
- Provide samples when requested
- Follow up after submission
- Build relationships even if you don't win

---

### Q: Should I respond to every RFQ?

**A:** Focus on RFQs that:
- Match your product capabilities
- Fit your production capacity
- Align with your pricing strategy
- Are from credible buyers
- You can realistically fulfill

**Quality over quantity** - Better to submit fewer, well-prepared quotes than many rushed ones.
