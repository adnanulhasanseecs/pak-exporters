## Introduction

Starting your export journey on Pak-Exporters is simple and straightforward. This step-by-step guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started and start connecting with global buyers.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

![Account Registration](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Account+Registration)

### What to Do

1. Visit [Pak-Exporters.com](https://pak-exporters.com)
2. Click the **"Register"** button in the top right corner
3. Select **"Supplier"** as your account type
4. Fill in your basic information:
   - Company name
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Contact number
5. Accept the terms and conditions
6. Click **"Create Account"**

### What Happens Next

- You'll receive a verification email
- Click the verification link in the email
- Your account will be activated
- You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Tips

- Use a professional email address (not personal Gmail)
- Choose a strong password
- Keep your login credentials secure
- Complete email verification immediately

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Company Profile

![Company Profile Setup](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Company+Profile+Setup)

### What to Include

**Basic Information:**
- Company logo (high-quality, square format)
- Company description (compelling, 200-500 words)
- Year established
- Number of employees
- Annual export volume (optional)

**Contact Information:**
- Business address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Website (if available)
- Social media links

**Business Details:**
- Main product categories
- Production capacity
- Quality certifications
- Export history
- Key markets

### Why It Matters

A complete profile:
- Builds trust with buyers
- Improves search visibility
- Shows professionalism
- Increases inquiry rates

### Tips

- Be honest and accurate
- Highlight your unique strengths
- Include relevant certifications
- Use professional language
- Update regularly

---

## Step 3: Choose Your Membership Tier

![Membership Selection](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Membership+Tiers)

### Your Options

**Starter** - Perfect for testing the platform
- Up to 10 product listings
- Basic features
- Affordable pricing

**Silver** - For established businesses
- Up to 50 product listings
- Enhanced features
- Better visibility

**Gold** - For serious exporters
- Unlimited listings
- Premium features
- Maximum visibility

**Platinum** - For industry leaders
- All Gold features
- Dedicated support
- Top placement

### How to Choose

- **New exporters:** Start with Starter
- **Established businesses:** Consider Silver or Gold
- **Large exporters:** Platinum offers maximum benefits

### Tips

- You can upgrade anytime
- Start small, scale up as you see results
- Compare features before deciding
- Consider your product count needs

---

## Step 4: List Your Products

![Product Listing](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Product+Listing+Interface)

### What to Include

**Product Information:**
- Product name (clear, descriptive)
- Detailed description (200-500 words)
- Key specifications
- Materials and quality standards
- Packaging details

**Images:**
- Main product image (high-quality, 1200x1200px)
- Additional images (different angles, uses)
- Minimum 3-5 images per product
- Professional photography recommended

**Pricing:**
- Unit price
- Currency (USD, EUR, etc.)
- Minimum order quantity
- Volume discounts (if applicable)
- Payment terms

**Categories and Tags:**
- Select appropriate category
- Add relevant tags for search
- Include keywords buyers might use

### Tips

- Use high-quality images
- Write detailed, accurate descriptions
- Include all relevant specifications
- Set competitive prices
- Update listings regularly

## Step 5: Respond to RFQs

![RFQ Response](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=RFQ+Response+Interface)

### How It Works

1. **Monitor RFQs** - Check new postings daily in your categories
2. **Review Requirements** - Read buyer needs carefully
3. **Submit Quote** - Provide competitive, detailed quote
4. **Follow Up** - Contact buyer if they have questions
5. **Build Relationship** - Even if you don't win, maintain contact

### What to Include in Your Quote

- Competitive pricing
- Product specifications
- Quality assurance details
- Delivery timeline
- Payment terms
- Your unique advantages

### Tips

- Respond quickly (within 24-48 hours)
- Be competitive but realistic
- Highlight your strengths
- Provide samples when requested
- Follow up professionally
