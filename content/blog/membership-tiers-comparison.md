## How to Choose the Right Tier

### Consider Your Business Stage

**New Exporter:**
- Start with **Starter** to test the platform
- Evaluate results after 2-3 months
- Upgrade when you see success

**Established Business:**
- Consider **Silver** or **Gold** based on product count
- Evaluate your growth goals
- Choose based on feature needs

**Industry Leader:**
- **Gold** or **Platinum** for maximum visibility
- Consider dedicated support needs
- Evaluate ROI on premium features

### Consider Your Product Count

- **1-10 products:** Starter
- **11-50 products:** Silver
- **51+ products:** Gold or Platinum
- **Extensive catalog:** Gold or Platinum

### Consider Your Goals

**Testing the Platform:**
- Start with Starter
- Upgrade after seeing results

**Scaling Your Business:**
- Silver or Gold depending on size
- Focus on features that drive growth

**Maximum Visibility:**
- Gold or Platinum
- Invest in premium placement

## Upgrade Path

You can upgrade your membership at any time:

1. **Starter → Silver:** When you need more listings
2. **Silver → Gold:** When you want premium features
3. **Gold → Platinum:** When you need dedicated support

**Benefits of Upgrading:**
- Immediate access to new features
- Prorated pricing for remaining period
- No disruption to existing listings
- Seamless transition

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change my membership tier?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

### What happens to my listings if I downgrade?

If you downgrade to a tier with fewer allowed listings, you'll need to remove excess listings or upgrade again.

### Do I get a refund if I upgrade?

Upgrades are prorated, meaning you only pay for the remaining period at the higher tier rate.

### Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Start with Starter membership to test the platform, then upgrade when you're ready.
